Ready Central

Track drivers, readiness steps, simple calculations, and logs — stored locally in your browser. Export/import lets you back up data or share with a dispatcher/manager.

What this app does

  • Save drivers (identifier, license/med dates, notes)
  • Run a readiness checklist before rolling
  • Simple readiness score calculator
  • Log activity (inspections, docs, issues)
  • Export/import JSON + download CSV for logs

Important note

Data is stored in this browser only (localStorage). If you switch devices/browsers, you’ll want to use Export/Import.

About Drivers

Drivers are the backbone of execution. Ready Central is designed to help drivers stay organized and prepared — reducing last-minute surprises and delays.

Driver-first focus

  • Clear readiness steps that are fast on mobile
  • Documentation reminders (med card, license, inspections)
  • Consistent check habits that improve safety and reliability
  • Simple logs that help dispatch/ops understand what’s happening

About Ready Central

Ready Central is a centralized readiness hub for drivers and teams. The goal is simple: fewer delays, fewer unknowns, smoother handoffs.

Why it exists

Small missing details cause big delays: documents, equipment issues, unclear load status, and miscommunication. Ready Central makes readiness visible.

What you can build next

  • Photo uploads (docs/inspection)
